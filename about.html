Porsche 911

About Porsche 911

The Porsche 911 is a high-performance sports car manufactured by German automobile manufacturer Porsche AG. Introduced in September 1964, the 911 has a distinctive design and a powerful, rear-mounted, flat-six engine, which has been continuously refined over the years.

The 911 is known for its excellent handling, precision engineering, and performance capabilities, making it a favorite among car enthusiasts and racing drivers alike. Available in various models and trims, the 911 offers a blend of luxury, performance, and engineering excellence.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Flat-Six
  • Color Options: Guards Red, Carrera White, Jet Black, Racing Yellow, Lava Orange
  • Model Variants: Carrera, Carrera S, Targa 4, Turbo, GT3
  • Alloy Wheels: 20-inch and 21-inch options available
